Vocabulary

  1. Fairtrade-labeled 公平交易，互惠贸易
    a category of products for which farmers from developing countries have been paid an officially agreed fair price.
    来自发展中国家的农民以保护价出售农产品。

Course

表格题
  1. 数据分组
    趋势、大小、占比
    从大到小，先写英国，变化最大底数最大两个数值都不可少。
    瑞士：变为两倍，doubled, twice，至少给出一个起点或者终点。
    比利时：增幅较大
    最后两个增加了相同的量。rose/increase
    • The sales of coffee in Denmark and Sweden rose by 0.2 million euros.
    • The sales of coffee in Denmark and Sweden had a rise of 0.2 million euros.
    • increase by the same amount as…
      动态表达

$;$
2. Example

  • The first table shows low-level coffee sales increasing in all five countries, albeit to widely varying degrees.
  • In two places sales increased by the same small amount: from 1.8 to 2 million euros in Denmark, and from …
  • The increment(增加) was slightly larger in Belgium, from 1 to 1.7 million euros.
  • Meanwhile, in Switzerland sales doubled from…
  • Finally in the UK there was an enormous increase, from … to …(the most popular)
大作文
种类
  • 议论文
    是否同意类，利弊分析类，双边讨论类
  • 说明文
    • causes/reasons+solutions/measures;
    • Problems/effects+solutions/measures;
  • 混合类
    议论文一问+说明文一问
是否同意类
  • Do you agree or disagree?
  • To what extent do you agree or disagree?
  • How far do you agree or disagree?

可能的立场：
问法1:
完全同意，完全不同意
问法2，3:
（偏向性的立场）
完全同意，完全不同意，部分同意

文章的架构：

  1. 完全（不）同意
    主体1: (不)同意的理由1
    主体2: (不)同意的理由2
    主体3: (不)同意的理由3/让步段
  • 让步段的写作(主体段1或者3)：
    • 连接词：
      Admittedly, Certainly, Granted, It is undeniable that…+反方观点
    • 呈现反方观点
    • 分析反方观点的合理性
    • 找出解决方案进行驳斥
    • 回归我方观点
    • example：
      看电视对小孩有害；
      视力下降；影响学业；电视中的暴力情节影响身心发展；
      家长限制看电视时间；
      适当观看是有益的；
  1. 部分同意
    主体1: 同意部分的论证（2-3个理由）
    主体2: 不同意部分的论证（2-3个理由）

主体段的论证结构：
I–E–E

  • I:idea/topic sentence（主题句，放在主体段的第一句）
  • E:explanation（解释说明，对主题句中抽象性语言文字进行paraphrase即同义词替换）
  • E:evidence论证
    举例论证、对比论证、假设论证、因果论证
  • 解释说明：
    对上文信息尤其是中心论点进行转述改写。
    example：
    中心论点：不得不承认科学突破带来的教育改革让传统的教学模式暴露出许多问题。
    科学突破：电子产品的普及和4g网络在主要国家的覆盖
    传统的教学模式：讲座
    解释说明的衔接：This is to say/in other words/To put in another way/By this I mean that;
  • 因果论证：
    表达原因的衔接：
    表达结果的衔接：in light of/in view of/because of this
  • 举例论证：
    举例论证避免个例，应该普适性、说服力、考官识别度
    个例——模糊化
    individuals/people
    举例衔接：for example/instance/To put it simply/such sa/like/namely
  • 对比论证：
    对比衔接：
  • 假设论证：
    虚拟语气；if only/providing that

作业：思考+主体段

主体段：

In terms of the sales of banana, it seemed that banana is the most popular in the UK among the five countries, with its sales growing from 15 million to 47 million euros.

Sales of banana in Switzerland witnessed a dramatic increase during the two years, starting from 1 million and ending at 5.5 million euros, which is the same as the sales in Denmark that experienced a rise from 0.6 milliom euros before reaching 4 million euros in the sdecond year.

On the contrast, the sales of banana in Belgium and Sweden showed a sharp drop from about 2 million to about 1 million euros.

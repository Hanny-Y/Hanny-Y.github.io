大作文的解题步骤

  1. 判断问法
  2. 划关键词（TR）
    实义词n, adj, v,
    动作的主体：children, teenagers, adults, people
    When choosing a job, the salary is the most important consideration.To what extent do you agree or disagree?
  3. 立场的判定
    对于题干中的绝对化字眼的词，一般不建议论证其成立。
    绝对化的词：the most/the best/only/all/any
    部分同意：工资重要但不是最重要
    部分不同意——不同意题干中绝对化字眼的词。
    保持人称的一致性；避免使用you人称代词
    文章结构：
    主体1:同意部分的论证
    Salary/wage/income/finacial factors/what is earned/ what we earned from work plays an important role/is essential/is crucial in daily life.(主题句)
    主体2:不同意部分的论证
    • Other factors/motivation are of equal importance/equally important.(主题句)
    • working atmosphere; environment; relationship with colleagues and leaders; welfare; pressure from work; promotion chance;stability; location
    • What is earned is the main source of income for most family.
      pay for the bills–clothing, food, housing, transportation, education, health care–guarantee quality of life.（因果+举例）
    • If income is ignored/ not taken into consideration/account–cannot make ends meet/support oneself financially/make a living.（假设）
    • Some artists including musicians and painters focus on interests and hobbies—struggle to make a living and fail to support their family financially.（举例）

是否同意类开头段：

背景信息+题干改写+个人立场呈现
背景信息：若题干中无，则需改写
时间状语：over the past years; over the decades; the past years have witnessed
古今对比：in the past; nowadays
追述原因：On account of
个人立场的呈现：
I insist/argue/claim/assert that…
I hold the belif/niew that…
from my point of view, …
from my perspective, …
from my viewpoint, …
Personally, I believe…
As far as i am concerned, …

结尾段

In conclusion, …+主体段总结概括+重申个人立场
To conclude, …
不使用in a nutshell因为不够正式

give a priority to sth
proritize sth

主题句： On the one hand, I agree that money is necessary in order for people to meet their basic needs.
支持句： For example, we need money to pay for housing, food, bills, health care and education. Most people consider it a priority to at least earn a salary
